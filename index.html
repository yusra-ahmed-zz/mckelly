Our Story

Tim has a way with words and charmed Michelle on Tinder. Their first date was at Freehouse before walking to Whole Foods for dessert of Mochi. Michelle’s nickname has been “Mochi” ever since. After dating for almost a year, Michelle made the move from Uptown to live in the North Loop with Tim and his lovably grumpy shih apso, Lando. Upon moving in together, Michelle and Tim rescued their quirky little shih tzu, Opal.

When not trying new restaurants, Tim and Michelle can be found at the movies, at home watching reality TV shows, or studying the law. Tim is the cook in the relationship, specializing in Italian cuisine. A famous favorite is his baked ziti. On occasion, Michelle will make lefse for Tim. Tim originally intended to propose on a trip to Miami, but bumped up his timeframe by a whole two weeks just so he could brighten the mood on election night.

Bridal Party

Matt—Man of Honor

Mattie is the bride’s younger brother and one of her best friends. From childhood to adulthood Michelle has always had Mattie by her side. He has been dragged to dance competitions, played sous chef in Michelle’s “baking shows,” been paid 10 cents to be a Diet Coke runner, and been in numerous situations together with their quirky parents. There is no better brother than Mattie and Michelle is so lucky to have him!

Yusra—Maid of Honor

Yusra is Michelle’s partner in crime. There is no one who is up for Michelle’s shenanigans quite like Yusra. They met in the dorms and after they got to know each other were fast friends. From obsessively going over AIM conversations, sharing a creepy attic apartment together, finding ways to procrastinate on papers, to running through the streets of Uptown, no friend truly knows Michelle quite like Yusra. Although they are both dramatic sometimes, Michelle and Yusra have so much fun together. Michelle is so happy that she has Yusra in her life!

Jenna—Bridesmaid

Jenna is one of Michelle’s oldest and dearest friends. The two of them have been through so much together. During the awkward teenage years, they embarrassed themselves so many times (rolling on the floor in front of Varsity… There’s someone doing “drugs” in the bathroom… just to name a few). The adult years have honestly been pretty awkward too. As much fun as Michelle and Jenna have together, they have also supported each other through a lot. Jenna is one of Michelle’s deepest confidantes. There is no friend quite like Jenna!

Rochelle—Bridesmaid

Rochelle (affectionately known as Roch) and Michelle met in college. They quickly bonded over their dislike of awkward house parties even creating “the awkward corner.” Through the years, Roch has been such an amazing friend to Michelle. She is the best giver of advice and is always one to help Michelle navigate a situation. Some of the best times with Roch have been spending summers walking the lake, grabbing happy hour as a break from busy lives, and finally hosting parties of their own so no “awkward corner” is needed. Roch is such an amazing friend to have!

Reva—Bridesmaid

Reva and Michelle met in college and quickly began to have so much fun together. The two always have such a good time when they are together. From travelling to Italy (and getting stuck in the Rome airport) to just hanging out in yoga pants drinking wine at someone’s apartment, Reva and Michelle always have fun. Reva has become such a trusted friend that Michelle can tell anything to. Reva truly is such a special friend to have!

Rima—Bridesmaid

Rima and Michelle met in college and were roommates for a few years. Rima is such a fun friend to have. Some of the best memories Michelle has are of late nights spent out dancing with Rima, throwing WOP parties at their apartment, watching Bollywood, and staying up in their apartment having late night chats. Throughout the years, Rima has always been there for Michelle. She is such a great friend to have!

Heather—Best Man

Everyone should be so lucky as to have a friend like Heather. Always ready with coffee, a yoga mat, and a listening ear, Heather’s supported Tim through the ups, downs and in-betweens. With three simple words of advice (“Get Tindin’, yo”), Heather Maurer made this entire website possible.

Ryan—Groomsman

If Heather’s the “Best Man,” Ryan would most assuredly describe himself as “the Better Man.” That’s a Wedding Singer joke, which is apt as Ryan understands Tim’s pop-culture references. Whether scaring people at the Dead End Hayride or rolling noobs in Destiny 2, there are few people Tim would rather have at his side.

Mike—Groomsman

Tim’s close friend for over twenty years, Mike is a master of witticisms and LEGOs. A bond forged in the fourth grade has continued in the form of frequent political chats, pop culture knowledge dumps, and spirited texts about pro wrestling. It’s the rare day where Tim and Mike don’t speak.

Brandon—Groomsman

Michelle’s cousin, Brandon grew up as an older brother figure to her. Few people have been through life with Michelle like Brandon has. Brandon loves to take advantage of Michelle’s gullibility and he and Tim have had a few laughs at her expense. He is such a jokester, always making people laugh. All jokes aside, Brandon is one of the closest people to Michelle and has become a good friend to Tim as well. They are both so lucky to have him in their lives!

Kevin—Groomsman

Few people make Tim laugh like Kevin Crampton. Friends since peewee foot ball, Tim has watched “The Fish” grow from a tiny aquatic lifeform into a damn fine man. In high school, Kevin and Tim shepherded their intramural basketball dynasty to dead last. It’s been uphill ever since.

Matt—Groomsman

The Gru-bear has many names. The team wildcard, Matt Gruber is a man of mystery. Tim and Gruber have shared many exciting adventures over the years, none of which they can legally speak about at this time.
